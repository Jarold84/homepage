About Me

Hi, everybody!

I’ve spent the past 8 years working in the animation industry where I learned a surprising breadth of skills. I began as a Technical Director, leveraging my strong background in computer science to maintain a diverse set of departmental tools. I worked closely with artists to create custom interfaces and scripts in a variety of software packages.

From there, I transitioned into a more artistic role as a compositor where I coordinated with many departments to create theater-ready final images. In this position, I had the opportunity to develop strong problem solving skills in all parts of the animation pipeline. Our strict schedule paired with the demand for a high quality visual product required a deep knowledge of our toolset and an open mind.

My responsibilities grew significantly in the past few years as I took over the position of team lead. I managed a group of 5-15 artists, a compositor, and a technical director on a feature length animated film. I worked closely with other department supervisors to schedule our workload, gave creative feedback on the artists’ work, and facilitated the technical growth of the members on my team.

Though I’ve spent a lot of time in the film industry, I am excited to return to my first passion: software development. I began my career as a user interface developer and found myself enamored with the process. I loved working closely with clients to understand their needs, then implementing smart solutions to their problems. I believe that my experience in the animation industry has only strengthened my creativity and appreciation of the power of color, composition, and form across all varieties of media.

I am confident that I can offer you the technical, leadership, and problem-solving skills you are looking for. Feel free to contact me! Thanks for your time.

Sincerely,

Jason Arold
